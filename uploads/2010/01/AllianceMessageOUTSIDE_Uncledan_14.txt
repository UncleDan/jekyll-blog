Welcome to the [color=#ffff12][b]Rashkan Homeworld Alliance[/b][/color], one of the fastest growing alliances in the [color=#068dd1][b]Federation[/b][/color]. Membership is open to all respectable pilots who wish to join, with the idea of helping each other for the better good of the [color=#068dd1][b]Federation[/b][/color] and of our sectors.

[color=#ffff12][b]We[/b][/color] are looking for those who wish to achieve better goals within the great game of the Universe and become good [color=#068dd1][b]Federal Citizens[/b][/color].

[color=#ffff12][b]We[/b][/color] are aiming to encourage our members to progress efficiently forming a good background under honorable principles, by developing you and your buildings, within [url=http://pardus.butterfat.net/pardusmapper/maps/orion/Rashkan.html]Rashkan[/url], [url=http://pardus.butterfat.net/pardusmapper/maps/orion/Betelgeuse.html](West) Betelgeuse[/url], [url=http://pardus.butterfat.net/pardusmapper/maps/orion/PassFED-13.html]Pass FED-13[/url] and [url=http://pardus.butterfat.net/pardusmapper/maps/orion/JO4-132.html]JO 4-132[/url] as systems. Incentives for new pilots combined with their commitment to development will be given and our experienced members are here to assist you in your development needs. For any alliance membership information please contact our [color=#ffff12][b]Application Manager [url=http://orion.pardus.at/sendmsg.php?to=Turtle Dude]Turtle Dude[/url][/b][/color].

[color=#ffff12][b]We[/b][/color] also built, own and maintain the [color=white][b]Military Outpost Wall[/b][/color] in [url=http://pardus.butterfat.net/pardusmapper/maps/orion/PassFED-13.html]Pass FED-13[/url], the northern gateway into the [color=#068dd1][b]Federation Rashkir Cluster[/b][/color]. During times of low diplomatic occurrences the Alliance will defend its sectors and passes and  those in opposition to the diplomatic decline are requested to not enter our sectors or passes, or no compensation will be given to unnecessary deaths. For any pass issue please contact our [color=#ffff12][b]Defence Manager [url=http://orion.pardus.at/sendmsg.php?to=Turtle Dude]Turtle Dude[/url][/b][/color].

[color=#ffff12][b]We[/b][/color] hold a no-tax policy apart from times of need, for defence of our and our neighbours sectors.
